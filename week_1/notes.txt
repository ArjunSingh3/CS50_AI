Convertion to CNF(Conjunctive NOrmal Form)
    * Eliminate Bi-Conditonals:
        * turn (a<->b) into (a->b)^(b->a)
    * Eliminate implications:
        * turn (a->b) into ~a U b
    * Move ~ inwards using De Morgan's Laws
        * eg. turn ~(a^b) into ~a U ~b
    * Use Distributive law to distribute U wherever possible

    eg. (P U Q) -> R
        _______________
        ~(P U Q) U R
        _______________
        (~P ^ ~Q) U R
        _______________
        (~P U R) ^ (~Q U R)
